# Executive Report Example

## Sample Summary

Organization readiness is currently **Moderate** with an overall score of **68/100**.
The most material risk drivers are response runbook maturity, tabletop execution cadence, and third-party incident escalation consistency.

## Key Findings

- Incident response playbooks exist but are not consistently tested
- Detection-to-containment workflow is partially documented
- Vendor escalation paths are defined but not validated quarterly

## Priority Actions (30-60-90 Days)

### 30 Days

- Finalize role-based incident runbooks
- Establish severity matrix and ownership handoffs

### 60 Days

- Execute cross-functional tabletop exercise
- Remediate high-severity process gaps identified in scoring

### 90 Days

- Implement recurring readiness review cycle
- Track trend metrics and present to executive stakeholders

## Executive Takeaway

The organization has a workable baseline but needs operational hardening to improve repeatability under real incident conditions.
Targeted process improvements can raise readiness and reduce response volatility over the next quarter.
